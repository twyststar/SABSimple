Canada is one of the top international student destinations in the world.

With one of the longest coastlines, vast wilderness, world-class cities, and a culture of tolerance and diversity, Canada is a natural destination for thousands of international students. One of the biggest reasons students choose to come to Canada is the quality of a Canadian education. A Canadian degree is widely recognized as equal to one from the United States, Australia, or the United Kingdom.

Canada is one of the safest countries in the world, with a low crime rate. In terms of social progress and development, according to Global News, Canada was ranked the second country in the world with the highest quality of life.

In Canada, students can pleasantly combine an enriching academic life with diverse and entertaining leisure activities.

English and French are both official languages in Canada and share equal status, but anywhere you choose to go in Canada, you can easily survive just by speaking and communicating in English.

Gulf Islands Secondary School

The Gulf Islands International Program is truly committed to ethnic diversity and the synergy that results when young people from around the globe work together to create a better world. Given our strong emphasis on intellectual and social development, the Gulf Islands International Program is a natural choice for highly motivated students who value a commitment to academic and personal development.

The commitment to cultural diversity has resulted in the creation of four distinct programs that are designed to meet the specific educational and social needs of the international students who enroll in the Gulf Islands International Program:Â 

  • Academic Study and Graduation Program
  • English as a Second Language Program
  • Cultural Awareness Program
  • Specialty Programs

Gulf Islands Secondary School boasts a reputation for academic excellence, student support, winning sports teams, outstanding performing and visual arts, and exceptional computer technology programs, including computer animation, robotics, webpage design, computer programming, computer assisted drafting, film and photography.
