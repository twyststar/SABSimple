Why Choose Study Abroad Network

My name is Alexandra Anderson and I am the owner of Study Abroad Network.

I started working with High School students wanting to study abroad over 7 years ago. Coming from the travel industry, I was very excited to have the opportunity to help find the perfect study abroad program and places for High School students.

My first experience with students was working for an Agency in Germany. We sent students to study in Canada, the United States, New Zealand and Australia. After I moved to the USA, I developed a Canada High School program for a large international Education Company.

Last year, I decided to get away from large student numbers and focus on personalized service for each student wanting to study abroad.

I will be your personal guide, from when you first contact me, through the program's completion. I will guide you through the school selection and the application process, I will assist with preparation for departure, and provide support while you are away from home.

It can be challenging to find the right Agency or person to help you make a good decision about your future school and location. I have travelled to many places and schools in Canada and the USA, always staying in close contact with the schools and students to provide a personalized service. I might be the right advisor for you.

I speak English and German.

My focus is to find the perfect location for you to experience an unforgettable adventure studying abroad.
