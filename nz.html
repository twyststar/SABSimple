Aotearoa

Safe, friendly, and welcoming, New Zealand is a natural playground full of opportunities for unique experiences.

New Zealand’s practical, hands-on style of learning encourages creative thinking rather than learning by memorizing.

You’ll gain a better understanding of different places and people by interacting with new cultures, such as New Zealand’s unique Māori culture.

New Zealand’s relaxed pace of life gives you the time to enjoy everything our vibrant modern cities have to offer. Enjoy live music, theater, film, dance, free festivals or international sports fixtures, or hang out with your new friends at a cafe, restaurant or bar.

Most of our cities and towns are relatively small and uncrowded, making your daily commute easier and faster than back home. Wherever you live, you won’t be far from beaches, parks, walking trails and cycle tracks.

Our Schools

We partner with Outdoor Education New Zealand to offer a wide selection of excellent schools spanning both the North and South islands.

"We offer international students an incredible learning experience of studying in New Zealand’s outdoor adventure-land, the ODENZ schools have a particular strength in outdoor education and offer challenging practical programmes designed to encourage personal and social development."
